Artist Quote

R. Crumb, in full Robert Crumb, (born August 30, 1943, Philadelphia,
Pennsylvania, U.S.), American counterculture comic book artist and
social satirist, known for his distinctive artwork and excellent marriage
of drawing and narrative and for creating such well-known characters
as Fritz the Cat and Mr. Natural.
